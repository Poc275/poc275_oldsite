USA & Canada 2009

This site is a commemoration of a trip to the United States and Canada in June and July of 2009. This amazing trip took in visits to New York City, Washington D.C, Toronto (including Niagara Falls), and Boston. The site highlights the possible activities, basic information and points of interest of each city in the hope that it can be of use to people planning a similar trip.
